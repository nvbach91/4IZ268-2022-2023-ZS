Career with Commerzbank

Shape the future of banking with us

Digitisation offers us great opportunities to meet the wishes and needs of our customers in a better and faster way. But to achieve this, we also have to change: We are leaving the traditional bank behind us and are choosing to move forward as a digital enterprise. And this is exactly why we need talented people who will join us on this journey. From apprentices to Graduate Trainees to professionals - from Relationship Managers for our customers to lateral thinkers for our ideas, talented individuals with idea who support us with their expertise in times of digitisation and continuous change.

Our service in return: ample space for professional and personal development, work-life balance through innovative working time models and a distinctive team culture.

We will make life easier for our customers - digitally and personally. Are you in?

Students

What do you want? Finally getting down to business!

Lectures, seminars, term papers, exams & degrees - for years you have learned what other clever minds have done, said and developed. Now it's time to design it yourself. We are leaving the traditional bank behind us to develop into a digital enterprise. On the journey towards banking of the future, we are looking for lateral thinkers like you - with unconventional ideas and creative drive. In return, we offer you the freedom and responsibility to prove yourself via these challenges.

Now the question is: Are you still in the middle of your studies or are you about to graduate?

We have a lot of offers and challenges for you. Get in!

During your studies:

  • ▸

    Internships: Are you currently studying for your Bachelor's/ Master's degree or have you recently graduated and want to take a closer look into real life banking? Then it’s about time you got to know us!

  • ▸

    Study Circle (applies to Germany only): Have you already demonstrated to us during your internship or apprenticeship, that you are highly skilled? Then our study circle programme is the perfect choice for you.

After your studies:

  • ▸

    Graduate Trainee programmes: In our Graduate trainee programmes, practice is anything but theoretical. Whether you are interested in a generalist or specialist programme, you can expect a wide range of opportunities during your employment .

Professionals

When it's time for something new

Changing perspectives are part of our daily business - to turn a traditional bank into a digital enterprise. As a new team member, you will support us with your expertise and create your own job with your own vision. Interested?

Compensation

Performance pays off at Commerzbank. Because those who are committed to working for us benefit from an attractive remuneration system. This enables us to attract and retain high-performing employees and contribute to the success of our company.

A solid basis

As a rule, anyone who joins us is paid accordingly to the collective wage agreement. The collective agreements for the private banking industry apply. Whether you are a junior staff member or an experienced specialist, we offer many other attractive benefits in addition to the collectively agreed remuneration!
