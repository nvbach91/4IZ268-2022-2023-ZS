Rogue Esports

Rogue is a reputable esports company with teams that compete in a variety of games in Europe and the US. A major squad that competes in the League of Legends European Championship (LEC) and an academy team that competes in Poland's national league, the Ultraliga, are its two League of Legends teams that are headquartered in Europe. Franklin Villarreal, Derek Nelson, and Carson Knuth created Team Rogue in 2016, and in 2018 ReKTGlobal and its partners bought the business. The London Royal Ravens of the Call of Duty League are another squad owned by ReKTGlobal. Famous DJ Steve Aoki and YouTuber Vikram "Vikkstar123" Barn share ownership of the company. Other investors include Landon Collins, Nicky Romero, Rudy Gobert, Imagine Dragons, and Nick Gross.

Rogue and KOI, an esports organization run by Ibai Llanos and Gerard Pique, announced their merger on October 6, 2022. The current Rogue teams will change their branding to KOI as a result of this.
