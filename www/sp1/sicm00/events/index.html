LEC 2023 | Format Changes Region Mergers & More

In order to commemorate the second decade of Riot Games, Riot has chosen to write a new chapter in the history of League of Legends Esport by combining the major European region with Turkey, the CIS, the Middle East, and Africa (MENA) to create the even larger region known as EMEA.

The League of Legends European Championship (LEC), which will soon merge with other competitions, will become the League of Legends EMEA Championship and bear the same acronym (LEC).

As you can see, the LEC is evolving in more ways than just its name and its merger. Additionally, we are considering a whole new format for the most prestigious competition in EMEA.
