KOI x Rogue Alliance

Esport organisations KOI and Rogue are merging for the upcoming season. Current LEC champions Rogue and their parent company Infinite Reality are teaming up with the effervescent Spanish esports organization KOI, which was founded by former LVP caster and Twitch celebrity Ibai Llanos and former professional footballer and founder of sports media investment company Kosmos Gerard Pique. Beginning in early 2023, the formidable strategic agreement will see the firms pooling their resources to rule esports throughout Europe.
