Rogue Becomes LEC Summer 2022 Champion

On September 11, in a rematch of the 2022 spring final, Rogue (RGE) and G2 Esports (G2) solidified their spots in the League of Legends European Championship (LEC) Summer 2022 finals. Rogue defeated G2 Esports in a resounding 3-0 way to win the LEC championship in front of a boisterous audience at the Malmo Arena in Sweden following a fierce best-of-five (BO5) series. The five Rogue players each contributed significantly to the triumph by following their teammates and working as a team.

Rogue defeated MAD Lions in the opening match of the upper bracket, winning 3-2, and went on to face G2 Esports in the semifinals. However, on September 3rd, G2 defeated Rogue 3-0 and eliminated the men in blue, knocking them to the lower bracket.

After that, Rogue entered the Malmo Arena feeling revitalized and ready to defeat legendary group Fnatic and eliminate the squad from the LEC Summer Finals. Rogue defeated G2 Esports in the finals as a result.
