Rogue LEC roster for 2022

Despite not exactly looking the same, Rogue's revamped League of Legends team will enter the following season with the same desire to win the LEC championship.

In addition to Odoamne, Larssen, and Trymbi for the 2018 Spring Split, the team formally revealed today that former DWG KIA replacement jungler Malrang and former Team Vitality AD carry Comp had signed on for 2022.
