Some key information about our dance school

History

Our founders were always disappointed by the quality of online dance platforms available on the internet.

Dance is such a big creative sphere with hundreds of different dance styles and a huge ammount of people interested and drawn to it. There's a countless ammount of dancers and choreographers all around the globe, that still are struggling to connect with each other and share their knowledge.

Dance is such a spere where sharing information, skills and experience with one another is the key part to become successful, grow and improve your skills.

That's why our founders decided to together on creating an easy-to-use, high quality international online dance platform that would be available to people in any part of the world.

You can either be a student and learn new styles and techniques from someone or you can become an instructor and share your knowledge and way of dancing with others(after a small background check and skillset check).

Our founders

Get to know the people who made it possible

Miki

Miki started dancing when she was only 3 years old. Her mom wanted her to be a professional ballerina, so she started with ballet. But soon enough Miki got a passion to more modern dance styles, starting with jazz modern she later became the best student in her dancehall team. Now it's her primary passion but she is also interested in growing in other styles but she could rarely take classes from the top choreographers as they all lived on a different side of the globe, that's what made her first think about creating the Ddance project

Dani

Dani on the other side discovered world of dance for himslef very recently. He used to do sports as a child but never was able to control his body as good as dancers do, that's why he thought of trying to try dancing. It doesn't need to be said that he immediately fell in love but the problem was that he used to live in a small town where there was no proper enviroment for learning to dance. That's how he came up with an idea of a website that would connect dancers and teachers independent of their location. Later on he found a like minded person Miki that he started to make this idea become reality.
