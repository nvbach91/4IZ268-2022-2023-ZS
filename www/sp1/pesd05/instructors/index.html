About us

Some key information about our dance school

Ara Cho

Ara Cho originally majored in childcare studies in college. In her Sophomore year, she happend to see a dance performance that inspired her to take up dancing as a hobby.

From 2009 to 2016, she performed in a dance team, and in 2017, she joined 1 MILLION as a choreographer/instructor. Ara Cho choreographed for Uhm Jung-Hwa, WJSN, Rocket Punch, and Chi pu (a Vietnamese singer). And she also has trained artists from various companies, such as SM, FENT.

Sasha

Alexander (Sasha) Vesely is a 21-year-old dancer from Valash. He is a member of the dance crew Dogga Clan and the Alotrie society.

He is engaged in choreography, but mostly dances improvisation, contemporary dance, breaking and experimental.

He is a part of the International Project People Power Partnership and has many dance performances and shows both in the Czech Republic and abroad. You can also see him in music videos (million+, Thom Artway, Most Wanted,...) or movie (Chevalier).

Wahe

Wahe is among the leading dancers and icons of the street dance scene. As a tutor, he has already taught countless lessons throughout the world, trained dozens of dancers and passed on their skills in the form of popular open classes in our studio.

Tomas Böckl

Tomas Böckl is a dancer, coach and choreographer at the F. A.C.T., Institute of TOG, AWA project, Opatow Flavours, JAD Dance Company and Dance Academy Prague.

He performed as a dancer at the Superstar contest, your face has a familiar voice and others attended countless dancer camps and workshops (HHM-Hiphopmeeting, SDK, Dance2xs camp, Choreo connection...). Choreographed for various competitions and shows (Hilton Prague NYE, etc.) in 2022, he participated in the production of BPM (Bohemian Gravity) at the National Theatre.

Adamson

Adam ADAMSON Kmenta - a young experienced dancer and lecturer who has been through a variety of dance training from an early age.

You can see him freestyling in battles for the Dogga clan crew, or performing on stage shows such as under the division, well, or dancing alternative theater projects for the Spolk collective.

Adam excites maximum energy in students during his lesson, and in hip hop choreographies you can expect fusion movement vocabulary, which Adam has.

Kajda

Kajda is a young energetic dancer and lecturer who knows how to properly and positively motivate and encourage those who are just starting out with style or dance.

She has her skillz not only from leading lecturers, such as Klara Marinovova, but also goes directly to Jamaica to educate herself, so she will give everyone who starts with dancehall the right answers to questions about technique, steps and culture from Jamaica.
