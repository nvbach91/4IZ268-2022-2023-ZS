About me and why I created this page

I am Dominik, playing card enthusiast and collector. I can do a few cardistry flourishes, however, I don't perform any magic because I like to be fascinated by it from the laymen spectator point of view. I enjoy taking photos of playing cards the most. It is fun trying to figure out where a certain design fits the best and to which props.

Given the information in the first paragraph, I think it makes sense to create a semestral project about playing cards. Having some fun while learning HTML and CSS is the best way for something to stick in my memory.
