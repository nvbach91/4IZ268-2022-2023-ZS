Choose your next 3D printing hardware!

Why?

3D printing and additive manufacturing is becoming more and more popular lately. Nowadays, it is not very easy to know what product is better, and it is getting more difficult to decide the right printer and materials.

There are many different types of additive manufacturing and 3D printing (especially FFF/FDM technology) is becomming much cheaper thus affordable and stable.

What?

Purpose of this website is to summarize all the important information about 3D printing hardware products.

The summary includes:

  • 3D printers
  • Filaments

The informations were taken from as many respected sources as possible, and also from the technical datasheets.

How?

A lot of information is publically available within the data sheets, but it may be difficult to understand and find the necessary data.

This website compares products and their important specifications and tries to explain the difference.

The website is trying to spread awareness of the 3D printing topics.
