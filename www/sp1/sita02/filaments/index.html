Filaments, 3D printing materials

The filament is like a fuel for your car. If you use low quality gas, the car won't be able to perform at 100%; also, the experience will be much worse. Therefore, it is the best to choose material with the best possible quality and precision.

If you are unsure which material to choose, feel free to check out the FAQ and other sources:

  • What 3D printing material is the best for beginner?
  • How to choose 3D printing filaments?

Main brands manufacturing in Czech Republic

Prusament

  • Tolerance: ± 0.02 mm on majority of materials
  • Ability to check the spool manufacturing process and precision via QR code on the spool.
  • Perfect spool winding.
  • Spool design with less plastic; recyclable.
  • Price: PLA 30 EUR/1kg
  • Available materials:
    • PLA, PETG, PVB, ASA, PC Blend, PA11-CF

Filament PM

  • Tolerance: ± 0.05 mm
  • Price: PLA 21.5 EUR/1kg
  • Available materials:
    • PLA, PLA+, PETG, ABS, ASA, FLEX, ABS-T, ASA, FLEX

Filamentum

  • Tolerance: ± 0.05 mm
  • Price: PLA 22 EUR/750g (~29 EUR/ 1kg)
  • Available materials:
    • OBC 905, Nonoilen, fluorodur, PLA Extrafill, PLA Crystal Clear, FlexFill TPE, FlexFill PEBA, CPE HG100, ASA ExtraFill, Timberfill, FlexFill TPU, PETG, Vinyl 303, Nylons, PC/ABS, PP 2320, CPE CF112 Carbon, ABS ExtraFill, HIPS
