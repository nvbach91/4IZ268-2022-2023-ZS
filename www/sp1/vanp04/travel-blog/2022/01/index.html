Sweden

Sweden, one of the Nordic countries, is a travel destination for a lot of people from around the world. Be it the beautiful nature, wealth, or nicely decorated and maintained environment, a lot of people talk highly about this country.

I have personally visited this country two times. One time was 10 years ago when I was still a child and didn't really pay attention to much of anything. The second visit I paid to this country was this year in the summer.

I would recommend anyone to visit this country, especially if money is not a problem for you. Since I am still a student, I could not spend as much as I would have wanted to. That was probably my only gripe with this country and especially Stockholm. Everything is just so pricey, from public transport tickets to food and drink. I would say that this country places among the priciest in Europe, topped by Switzerland and a few others.
