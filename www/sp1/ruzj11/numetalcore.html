Metalcore

Nu metalcore (sometimes called as new metal or metal revival), is a fusion between or metalcore as a metal [that came in 2010, being some bands influenced post-hardcore, deathcore, pop e R & B. The fusion is characterized by clean and guttural vocals typical of metalcore and deathcore, like rap and spoken word (typical of nu metal),guitars and basses with low tunings, in addition use of samples and turntables
