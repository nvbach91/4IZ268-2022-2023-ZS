Reds star in Brazil win

Manchester United’s Antony, Casemiro and Fred featured for Brazil as they started off their 2022 World Cup campaign with victory over Serbia. The Brazilians triumphed 2-0 at the Lusail Stadium in Qatar, ensuring that they put an early three points on the board in Group G, Which will surely help them to advance into the knockout stages. Tottenham forward Richarlison got both of the difference-making strikes in the second half, with the latter - an acrobatic effort - an instant Goal of the Tournament contender.

United midfielder Casemiro was the only one of United players named in the starting XI and played the full 90 minutes, impressing in his fifth match at a World Cup. The 30-year-old displayed a brilliant all-round performance for the favourites with a stunning shot late on, that cannoned away off the woodwork. In the second half, fellow Reds Fred and Antony were both introduced by Tite for their debuts at the tournament.

Alex Telles, who is currently on a season-long loan with Sevilla, awaits his World Cup bow having been an unused substitute in Lusail.

Brazil will hope to continue their perfect start to this year’s tournament when they face Switzerland on matchday two.
