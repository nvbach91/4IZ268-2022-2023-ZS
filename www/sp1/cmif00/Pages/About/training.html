Carrington Training Facility

Trafford Training centre or Carrington is our official training facility, this is the place, where all of the players train, eat and recover. The facility bagan It’s construction in 1999 at the request of Sir Alex Fergusson, because the old facility called The Cliff was no longer up to standart.In 2000 it replaced The Cliff as the official training facility for the United first team. In 2012 major expansions to the facility came such as a medical centre and a sports science department.

Carrington contains 14 training pitches, two of which are synthetic grass. There is the main building where most of the first team players do their activities. The main building has two floors. The bottom floor contains running tracks, rehabilitation hall, squatch and basketball courts, weights room, swimming pool, jacuzzi a sauna and a number of other useful rooms. The building also contains a restaurant, a number of offices, massage rooms and much more.
