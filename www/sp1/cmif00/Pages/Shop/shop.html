Museum and Stadium Tours

Stadium Tour

Take a closer look at Old Trafford in a way you have never experienced before. Visit the player changing rooms. See the stands from up close, take a look at the dugouts. You can even walk up to the pitch through the tunnel and imagine yourself as a player for Manchester United. On top of that there is an option to visit the Museum and learn about the club’s history and it’s accomplishments.

Legends Tour

Tour Old Trafford accompanied by iconic players. Relive the best moments, biggest victories and greatest upsets the best way possible. Accompanied by those who lived and breathed them. Join us on a once in a lifetime experience, a must for any true fan. You might meet heroes such as Lee Martin, Norman Whiteside and Alex Stepney.
