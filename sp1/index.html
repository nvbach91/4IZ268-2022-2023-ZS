Mappa (Animation Studio)

MAPPA Co., Ltd. (Japanese: 株式会社MAPPA, Hepburn: Kabushiki-gaisha MAPPA) is a Japanese animation studio headquartered in Suginami, Tokyo. It has produced anime works including Kids on the Slope, Hajime no Ippo Rising, Terror in Resonance, Yuri!!! on Ice, In This Corner of the World, Kakegurui, Banana Fish, Zombie Land Saga, Dororo, Dorohedoro, The God of High School, Jujutsu Kaisen and its prequel film Jujutsu Kaisen 0, Attack on Titan: The Final Season and Chainsaw Man.

MAPPA is an acronym for Maruyama Animation Produce Project Association.

David Waller 2022
